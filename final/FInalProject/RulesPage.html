The Rules of Rugby

Rugby has two major formats: Rugby Union and Rugby League. Below is a comparison of key rules:

Comparison of Rugby Union and Rugby League
Feature Rugby Union Rugby League
Number of Players 15 13
Game Duration 80 minutes 80 minutes
Field Size Larger Smaller
Scoring Tries, Conversions Tries, Goals
Substitutions 8 10
