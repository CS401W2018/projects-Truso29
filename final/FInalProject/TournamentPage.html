Major Rugby Tournaments

Rugby is home to several prestigious tournaments that attract global audiences with intense competition, national pride, and thrilling gameplay. Here are some closer looks at some of the most notable rugby tournaments:

Rugby World Cup

Held every four years for both men and women. This is the most prestigious international rugby tournament, showcasing the best teams from around the globe. The Webb Ellis Cup is awarded to the winner. New Zealand, South Africa, and Australia are historically dominant teams.

Six Nations Championship

An annual competition among European teams. Europe's premier rugby competition, known for its rich history and intense rivalries. The Triple Crown is a sub-prize for the Home Nations (England, Scotland, Ireland, Wales) if one team defeats the other three.

Rugby Championship

Features teams from the Southern Hemisphere. The top southern hemisphere tournament, known for fast-paced and high-intensity matches. The Bledisloe Cup (Australia vs. New Zealand) and the Freedom Cup (South Africa vs. New Zealand) are notable sub-competitions.

Major League Rugby

A growing competition in North America. It's a professional rugby union competition in North America that has been rapidly growing since its establishment in 2017.
